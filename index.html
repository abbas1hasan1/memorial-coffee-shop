Memorial Coffee Shop

✸ ✸ ✸

Your neighborhood coffee ritual, reimagined.

A Proposal for Greenside Memorial — Houston, Texas

The Concept

Memorial Coffee Shop is a neighborhood coffee house — not a chain outpost, not a fleeting trend. It's a place built around the daily ritual: the first sip that anchors your morning, the mid-afternoon pause that resets your day, the weekend linger over a pastry and a good conversation.


We believe Greenside Memorial deserves a gathering place with soul. A space where the baristas know your order, the light pours in through big windows, and every cup is crafted with intention. Warm, unhurried, and deeply local.

✸ ✸ ✸

Why Greenside

A Captive Community

Over 6 million square feet of Class-A office space lines the Katy Freeway corridor. Thousands of professionals at 10100 Katy Freeway and neighboring towers need a quality morning stop and afternoon escape — plus the growing residential base in Greater Memorial.

Mixed-Use Synergy

Coffee is the social anchor of every successful mixed-use development. It's the first door that opens and the last one to close — the heartbeat that draws foot traffic, builds community, and complements every neighboring tenant.

An Underserved Niche

The Memorial corridor is dotted with drive-through chains but lacks a dedicated craft coffee house — a real sit-down spot with specialty-grade beans, house-made pastries, and an atmosphere worth staying for. That gap is our opportunity.

The Experience

Walk in to warm wood surfaces, the hiss of steam wands, and daylight washing across communal tables. The espresso machine anchors the bar like a stage — every drink a small performance. Around you, soft music, the rustle of laptop keyboards, low conversation.


Natural materials, earth tones, and generous seating create a space that feels both elevated and effortless. Whether you're here for five minutes or five hours, it fits.

Community First

Local Houston Roasters

We'll partner with Houston's best specialty roasters — sourcing single-origin beans and custom blends that champion the city's thriving coffee scene and keep dollars in the community.

Neighborhood Board

A dedicated community board inside the shop: local events, artists, small businesses, and neighbors connecting. A physical social network for the Greenside community.

Co-Hosted Events

From morning run clubs to evening tasting sessions, we'll collaborate with Greenside tenants and the Memorial community to host events that make the development a true destination.

Sustainability Practices

Compostable cups and packaging, a bring-your-own mug discount, partnerships with local composting services, and a commitment to minimizing waste from day one.
